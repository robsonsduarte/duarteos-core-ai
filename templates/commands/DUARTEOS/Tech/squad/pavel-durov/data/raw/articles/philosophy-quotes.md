## TEMA 3: RESISTENCIA E CORAGEM

[DIRECT QUOTE] "The more pressure I get, the more resilient and defiant I become."
Fonte: Lex Fridman Podcast #482

[DIRECT QUOTE] "And I would rather lose everything I have than yield to this pressure because, if you submit to this pressure and agree with something that is fundamentally wrong and violates rights of other people as well, you become broken inside."

Fonte: Founder Boat / multiple interviews

## TEMA 7: DISCIPLINA PESSOAL E SAUDE

### 7 Coisas que Durov Abandonou

[DIRECT QUOTE] "In the last 15 years, I've had no alcohol, no caffeine, no meat, no pills, and no fast food."
Fonte: Canal Telegram pessoal

[DIRECT QUOTE] A lista completa de 7 coisas que nunca faz:
1. Alcool
2. Carne (peixe e permitido)
3. Qualquer tipo de remedios e medicamentos (exceto no dentista)
4. Nicotina e outras drogas viciantes
5. Cafe, cha preto e verde, energeticos (toma chas de ervas)
6. Fast food, acucar, refrigerantes
7. TV e suas alternativas
Fonte: Canal Telegram pessoal

[DIRECT QUOTE] "At certain points in life, I realized these things decrease productivity and clarity of thought. So I've been living without them for many years now and in general have been feeling great and healthy."
Fonte: Canal Telegram pessoal

[DIRECT QUOTE] "Health-wise it brought good results: I only had a fever once in the last 15 years."
Fonte: Canal Telegram pessoal

[DIRECT QUOTE] "A year ago I added more restrictions to my diet: no gluten, no dairy, no eggs, no fructose. I did it to achieve higher productivity and clarity of thought, as well as to train will power and self-discipline."
Fonte: Canal Telegram pessoal

[DIRECT QUOTE] "20 plus years of complete abstinence from alcohol, tobacco, coffee, pills, and illegal drugs."
Fonte: Lex Fridman Podcast #482

[DIRECT QUOTE] "Short-term pleasure isn't worth your future."
Fonte: Lex Fridman Podcast #482 / Founder Boat

### Alcool

[DIRECT QUOTE] "When you drink alcohol... your brain cells become paralyzed. They become literally zombies."
Fonte: Lex Fridman Podcast #482

[DIRECT QUOTE] "Why would you destroy this tool for short-term pleasure? This sounds ridiculous."
Fonte: Lex Fridman Podcast #482

### Acucar

[DIRECT QUOTE] "Sugar is addictive. Why consume it if you want efficiency and health?"
Fonte: Lex Fridman Podcast #482

### Exercicio Fisico

[DIRECT QUOTE] "I do 300 push-ups and 300 squats every morning. The main muscle is self-discipline."
Fonte: Lex Fridman Podcast #482

### Sono

[PARAPHRASE] Para Durov, sono e prioridade maxima. Ele escreveu que "Sleep promotes immunity, creativity and psychological well-being."
Fonte: Canal Telegram (aniversario de 37 anos, 2021)
